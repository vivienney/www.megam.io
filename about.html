About

Megam is an end-to-end open source datacenter automation bundled with PaaS along with cloud storage. We help to run your own IaaS, Storage, apps, services, containers (docker), analytics on your own cloud hosting.

Megam was founded in March 2013. We are a fun filled team of passionate open source enthusiasts located in chennai, India. Megam in tamil dialect stands for Cloud.
